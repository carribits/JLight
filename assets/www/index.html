ENERGY SAVING TIPS

Living Room

  • Switch light bulbs to CFLs
  • Unplug unused appliances or switch them off
  • Seal Windows and Doors to Reduce Drafts
  • Make sure all windows and doors are closed when the AC unit is on

Bedrooms

  • Turn off lights when you go to bed
  • Use energy saver light bulbs

Bathroom

  • Reduce Hot Water Use
  • Lower the temperature on your hot water heater

Washroom

  • Try to iron clothes once a week rather than everyday
  • Wash full loads when using the washing machine
  • Use shorter wash cycles for smaller load sizes
  • Use cold water to wash clothes
  • Use the sun to dry clothes when you can

Kitchen

  • Make sure the refrigerator is closed properly
  • Ensure the door seal on your oven and fridge are in good condition
  • Defrost food naturally instead of using the microwave
  • Avoid opening refrigerator doors too often
  • Leave space around the back of your fridge or freezer for air to circulate
  • Avoid placing your fridge or freezer next to your oven or stove
  • Use a gas stove instead of an electric stove

Home Office

  • Configure power plan software on computers
  • Switch computer monitors off when not in use
  • Switch off lights when room not occupied
  • Use daylight where possible
